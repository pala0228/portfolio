Project Overview

MVP Incentive System helps dealers to maintain their sales and track their incentives based on the sales. It also tracks their personal loan details and shows them up top 10 and bottom 10 performances of dealers for that quarter on their dashboard.

We built an admin system to set up configurations on incentives percentages and criterias for dealers to meet in order to avail incentives for their sales. System also helps to download reports for various aspects in order to see their performances.

We have migrated MVP Incentive System which was running on legacy platform with mainframes technology to Angular framework version 8 with Java as back end technology.
